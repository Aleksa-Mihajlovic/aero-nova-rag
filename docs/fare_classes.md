# AeroNova Airlines — Fare Classes

## Overview

AeroNova Airlines offers four fare classes: Basic, Standard, Flex, and Business.
Each fare class includes a different set of benefits, flexibility options, and baggage allowances.
Passengers should carefully review what is included before booking as upgrades after purchase may cost significantly more than booking the correct fare from the start.

## Basic Fare

Basic is the most affordable fare class offered by AeroNova Airlines. It is designed for passengers who travel light and do not require flexibility.

Basic fare includes one personal item (under-seat only), seat selection in designated Basic zones only (zones C and D, located toward the rear of the aircraft), and access to AeroNova's standard check-in procedures.

Basic fare does not include carry-on bin access, checked baggage, seat selection in premium zones, lounge access, or free rebooking. Changes to Basic fare tickets are not permitted. Cancellations on Basic fare are non-refundable. In cases of significant schedule changes initiated by AeroNova of more than 3 hours, Basic fare passengers are entitled to a full refund or free rebooking to the next available flight.

Basic fare passengers board in the last boarding group.

## Standard Fare

Standard fare is the mid-tier option and represents the best value for most passengers. It includes everything in Basic plus overhead bin carry-on access, one checked bag up to 23 kg, seat selection across all economy zones, and one free date change per booking (fare difference may apply).

Cancellations on Standard fare are eligible for an AeroNova Travel Credit equal to the ticket value minus a $40 cancellation fee. Travel credits are valid for 12 months from the date of issue and can be used toward any future AeroNova booking.

Standard fare passengers board in the second boarding group.

## Flex Fare

Flex fare is designed for passengers who need maximum flexibility. It includes everything in Standard plus two checked bags up to 23 kg each, free seat selection including extra legroom seats in economy (subject to availability), two free date or route changes per booking with no fare difference penalty, and same-day flight change option if seats are available on an earlier or later flight on the same route.

Cancellations on Flex fare are fully refundable to the original payment method, minus a $25 processing fee. Refunds are processed within 7 to 10 business days.

Flex fare passengers board in the first economy boarding group, immediately after Business passengers.

## Business Fare

Business fare provides access to AeroNova's premium cabin. The Business cabin features fully flat-bed seats on long-haul routes and extra-wide recliner seats on short and medium-haul routes. Seat pitch is a minimum of 60 inches on long-haul and 38 inches on short-haul.

Business fare includes three checked bags up to 32 kg each, priority check-in at dedicated Business counters, priority security lanes at participating airports, access to AeroNova partner lounges worldwide, premium meal service with a multi-course menu and complimentary alcohol, amenity kit on flights over 4 hours, and dedicated cabin crew.

Cancellations on Business fare are fully refundable with no processing fee. Date and route changes are unlimited and free of charge, subject to availability. If a Business passenger is involuntarily downgraded due to aircraft change or operational reasons, they are entitled to a 75% refund of the fare difference.

Business fare passengers board first before all other groups.

## Fare Upgrade Options

Passengers may upgrade their fare class after booking subject to availability. Upgrades from Basic to Standard cost $55 per flight. Upgrades from Standard to Flex cost $45 per flight. Upgrades from Flex to Business are priced dynamically based on route and availability and can be checked through Manage My Booking on the AeroNova website or app.

Bid upgrades to Business are available on selected routes. Passengers on Flex fare may submit a bid between 30 days and 24 hours before departure. Minimum bid amounts vary by route and are displayed during the bidding process. Successful bidders are notified between 24 and 48 hours before departure.

## Group Bookings

Groups of 9 or more passengers qualify for group booking rates. Group bookings must be made through the AeroNova Groups desk and are not available through the standard online booking engine. Group bookings on Standard fare or above receive a 10% discount on the base fare. Group bookings are subject to their own cancellation and change policies which will be outlined in the group contract provided at the time of booking.
