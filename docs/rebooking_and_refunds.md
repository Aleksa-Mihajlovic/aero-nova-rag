# AeroNova Airlines — Rebooking and Refunds

## Overview

AeroNova Airlines' rebooking and refund policies vary depending on the fare class
purchased. Passengers are strongly advised to review the conditions of their fare
before booking. In all cases where AeroNova initiates a significant change to a
flight, passengers are entitled to additional options regardless of their fare class.

## Voluntary Changes by Passenger

### Basic Fare
Basic fare tickets are non-changeable and non-refundable. Once purchased, the booking
cannot be modified by the passenger. If a Basic fare passenger is unable to travel,
the ticket has no residual value and cannot be transferred to another person or date.
The only exception is if AeroNova makes a schedule change of more than 3 hours to the
original departure time, in which case the passenger may rebook free of charge or
request a full refund.

### Standard Fare
Standard fare passengers are entitled to one free date change per booking. The change
must be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled departure. If the new flight has
a higher fare, the passenger must pay the fare difference. If the new flight has a
lower fare, no refund is issued for the difference. Additional changes beyond the first
free change are charged at $55 per change.

Cancellations on Standard fare are not refunded to the original payment method.
Instead, the passenger receives an AeroNova Travel Credit equal to the full ticket
value minus a $40 cancellation fee. Travel credits are issued within 5 business days
and are valid for 12 months from the date of issue.

### Flex Fare
Flex fare passengers may make up to two free date or route changes per booking.
Changes must be made at least 2 hours before departure. No fare difference penalty
applies, however if the new fare is higher the passenger must pay the difference.
If the new fare is lower a travel credit is issued for the difference.

Flex fare passengers may also make a same-day flight change to an earlier or later
flight on the same route if seats are available. Same-day changes must be requested
at least 2 hours before the original departure and are subject to seat availability.
No fee applies for same-day changes on Flex fare.

Cancellations on Flex fare are fully refundable to the original payment method minus
a $25 processing fee. Refund requests must be submitted through Manage My Booking or
by contacting customer support. Refunds are processed within 7 to 10 business days.

### Business Fare
Business fare passengers may make unlimited free date and route changes up to 1 hour
before departure. No fare difference penalty applies in either direction, however if
the new fare is higher the passenger must pay the difference. If the new fare is lower
a refund is issued to the original payment method for the difference.

Cancellations on Business fare are fully refundable to the original payment method
with no processing fee. Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

## Involuntary Changes by AeroNova

### Schedule Changes
If AeroNova changes the departure time of a flight by more than 3 hours, all passengers
regardless of fare class are entitled to one of the following options: free rebooking
to the next available AeroNova flight on the same route, free rebooking to an alternative
AeroNova route to the same destination, or a full refund to the original payment method.

Passengers must select their preferred option within 72 hours of receiving the schedule
change notification. If no selection is made within this period AeroNova will automatically
rebook the passenger onto the next available flight on the same route.

### Flight Cancellations by AeroNova
In the event of a flight cancellation by AeroNova, all passengers are entitled to a full
refund to the original payment method regardless of fare class. Alternatively passengers
may choose to rebook onto the next available AeroNova flight at no charge.

If the cancellation occurs within 14 days of departure and is not caused by extraordinary
circumstances, passengers may also be entitled to compensation under applicable aviation
regulations. Compensation amounts are determined by flight distance and are as follows:
flights under 1,500 km are eligible for $250 compensation, flights between 1,500 km and
3,500 km are eligible for $400 compensation, and flights over 3,500 km are eligible for
$600 compensation.

Extraordinary circumstances that exempt AeroNova from compensation obligations include
severe weather, air traffic control restrictions, security threats, and political instability
at the origin or destination airport.

### Denied Boarding
If a passenger is denied boarding due to overbooking, AeroNova will first seek volunteers
willing to take a later flight in exchange for compensation. Volunteers will receive a
travel credit of $300 plus free rebooking on the next available flight.

If insufficient volunteers come forward, passengers may be involuntarily denied boarding.
Involuntarily denied passengers are entitled to full compensation under aviation regulations
as described above, plus free rebooking on the next available flight or a full refund.

## How to Request a Refund or Rebooking

Refund and rebooking requests can be submitted through the following channels:
- Online via Manage My Booking at www.aeronova.com
- Through the AeroNova mobile app under My Trips
- By calling AeroNova customer support on +1 800 AERONOVA
- At any AeroNova airport desk

Processing times vary by channel. Online and app requests are typically processed fastest.
Airport desk requests may take longer during peak periods.

## Travel Credits

Travel credits issued by AeroNova are non-transferable and can only be used by the
passenger named on the original booking. Credits are valid for 12 months from the
date of issue unless otherwise stated. Credits cannot be exchanged for cash and have
no monetary value outside of the AeroNova booking system. Credits can be applied to
any AeroNova flight booking including taxes and fees.
