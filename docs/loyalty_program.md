# AeroNova Airlines — Loyalty Program (AeroRewards)

## Program Overview

AeroRewards is AeroNova Airlines' frequent flyer program. Membership is free and open
to all passengers aged 2 and above. Members earn points on every eligible AeroNova flight
as well as through purchases with AeroNova's partner network including hotels, car rentals,
and credit card partners.

Points can be redeemed for flights, upgrades, extra baggage, lounge access, and partner
rewards. Points do not expire as long as the account has at least one earning or redemption
activity within any 24-month period.

## Membership Tiers

AeroRewards has four membership tiers: Blue, Silver, Gold, and Platinum. All new members
start at Blue tier. Tier status is calculated based on Tier Qualifying Points (TQP) earned
within a calendar year running from January 1 to December 31. Status earned in a given year
is valid for the entire following calendar year.

### Blue Tier
Blue is the entry level tier and is awarded to all new members automatically. Blue members
earn 1 point per $1 spent on base fare. Blue members have access to the standard AeroRewards
redemption catalog and receive member-only promotional offers throughout the year.

### Silver Tier
Silver status is awarded to members who earn 10,000 TQP within a calendar year. Silver
members earn 1.25 points per $1 spent on base fare. Silver members receive priority
check-in at dedicated Silver and above counters, one complimentary standard seat upgrade
per year, and a 20% discount on extra baggage purchased at the airport.

### Gold Tier
Gold status is awarded to members who earn 25,000 TQP within a calendar year. Gold members
earn 1.5 points per $1 spent on base fare. Gold members receive all Silver benefits plus
complimentary lounge access for the member and one guest at all AeroNova operated lounges,
two complimentary upgrades to Flex fare per year, priority boarding regardless of fare class,
and a dedicated Gold member customer service line with reduced wait times.

### Platinum Tier
Platinum status is awarded to members who earn 50,000 TQP within a calendar year. Platinum
members earn 2 points per $1 spent on base fare. Platinum members receive all Gold benefits
plus unlimited complimentary lounge access for the member and up to two guests, four
complimentary upgrades to Business fare per year, guaranteed seat availability on any
AeroNova flight up to 24 hours before departure, a personal relationship manager for
bookings and special requests, and complimentary same-day rebooking on any route.

## Earning Points

Points are earned on eligible AeroNova flights based on the base fare paid excluding taxes
fees and surcharges. Bonus points may be earned on promotional fares or partner bookings.

Points are also earned through the AeroRewards partner network:
- AeroNova credit card partners: up to 3 points per $1 spent on everyday purchases
- Partner hotels: 1 point per $1 spent on room rate
- Partner car rentals: 500 points per rental
- AeroNova in-flight purchases: 1 point per $1 spent

Points from flights are credited to the account within 72 hours of the flight completing.
Partner points may take up to 30 days to appear. If points have not been credited after
these timeframes members should contact AeroRewards support with their booking reference
and receipt.

## Redeeming Points

Points can be redeemed through the AeroNova website, mobile app, or by calling the
AeroRewards service line.

### Flight Redemptions
The minimum redemption for a flight is 5,000 points for a one-way domestic flight.
International redemptions start at 15,000 points one-way. The number of points required
varies by route, season, and availability. Taxes and fees must always be paid in cash
and cannot be covered by points.

### Upgrade Redemptions
Passengers can use points to upgrade from economy to Flex fare for 3,000 points per
flight segment. Upgrades from economy to Business fare cost 8,000 points per segment
on short-haul and 18,000 points per segment on long-haul routes. Upgrades are subject
to availability and must be requested at least 24 hours before departure.

### Other Redemptions
Extra checked baggage: 2,000 points per bag per flight.
Lounge day pass: 4,000 points per person.
Partner hotel nights: vary by property, starting at 8,000 points per night.
Partner car rental: 3,000 points per rental day.

## Points Expiry and Account Activity

Points do not expire provided the account shows at least one qualifying activity within
any rolling 24-month period. Qualifying activities include earning points from a flight
or partner, redeeming points for any reward, or purchasing points through the AeroRewards
points purchase program.

If an account shows no activity for 24 consecutive months all accumulated points will
be forfeited without compensation. AeroNova will send a reminder email 90 days and
again 30 days before the expiry date to the email address registered on the account.
It is the member's responsibility to ensure their contact details are kept up to date.

## Transferring and Gifting Points

Points can be transferred between AeroRewards accounts at a rate of 500 points minimum
per transaction. A transfer fee of $10 applies per transaction regardless of the number
of points transferred. Points can be gifted to family members registered under the same
household profile at no charge. A household profile allows up to 6 members and must be
set up through the AeroRewards website.

## Program Terms and Conditions

AeroNova reserves the right to modify the AeroRewards program including point values,
redemption rates, tier thresholds, and partner offers at any time with 30 days notice
to members. In cases of program discontinuation members will be given a minimum of
6 months to redeem accumulated points. AeroNova is not responsible for points lost
due to account inactivity, incorrect contact details, or fraudulent activity.
