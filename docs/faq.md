# AeroNova Airlines — Frequently Asked Questions

## Booking and Payment

### What payment methods does AeroNova accept?
AeroNova accepts all major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, American
Express, and Discover. PayPal and Apple Pay are accepted through the mobile app and
website checkout. Bank transfers are accepted for group bookings only. AeroNova Travel
Credits can be applied at checkout for any booking. Cash payments are not accepted
online but are available at AeroNova airport ticket desks subject to a $15 handling fee.

### Can I book a flight for someone else?
Yes. You can book a flight for another person using your own payment method. The
passenger name on the booking must exactly match the name on the traveler's government
issued ID. Name corrections of up to 3 characters are permitted free of charge through
Manage My Booking. Full name changes are treated as a cancellation and rebooking and
are subject to the fare conditions of the original ticket.

### Is it cheaper to book directly with AeroNova?
Booking directly through the AeroNova website or app guarantees the lowest available
fare as AeroNova does not charge booking fees on direct bookings. Third party booking
platforms may charge their own service fees on top of the base fare. Additionally
managing your booking, requesting special assistance, and accessing AeroNova customer
support is significantly easier when booked directly.

### How far in advance can I book a flight?
AeroNova opens bookings up to 355 days in advance of the departure date. Last minute
bookings can be made up to 2 hours before departure for domestic flights and up to
3 hours before departure for international flights, subject to seat availability.

### Can I hold a fare without paying?
AeroNova offers a fare hold option on selected routes and fare classes. A fare can be
held for up to 24 hours for a non-refundable fee of $12. If the booking is completed
within the hold period the $12 fee is deducted from the total fare. If the hold expires
without a completed booking the fee is forfeited and the fare is released.

## Seats and Onboard Experience

### Can I choose my seat?
Seat selection is available for all passengers. Basic fare passengers may only select
seats in zones C and D at the rear of the aircraft free of charge. Standard and Flex
fare passengers may select any available economy seat free of charge. Business fare
passengers may select any Business cabin seat free of charge. Extra legroom seats in
economy are available for purchase by any passenger regardless of fare class for a
fee between $15 and $45 depending on the route and specific seat.

### What is the seat pitch in economy?
Standard economy seats on AeroNova short-haul flights have a seat pitch of 29 inches
and a width of 17 inches. On long-haul flights economy seats have a pitch of 31 inches
and a width of 17.5 inches. Extra legroom seats offer a pitch of 34 to 36 inches
depending on the aircraft type.

### Does AeroNova offer meals on board?
Complimentary meals are provided on all flights over 4 hours in economy and on all
Business fare flights regardless of duration. On flights under 4 hours a buy on board
menu is available with a selection of hot and cold snacks and beverages. Complimentary
water and a non-alcoholic beverage are provided on all flights regardless of duration.

Special meals including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher, and low-sodium
options are available on flights over 4 hours. Special meal requests must be made at
least 48 hours before departure through Manage My Booking.

### Is there WiFi on board?
WiFi is available on all AeroNova long-haul aircraft and on selected short-haul routes.
WiFi availability is indicated during booking and on the flight information page. Pricing
is as follows: messaging only plan $5 per flight, standard browsing plan $12 per flight,
and premium streaming plan $18 per flight. Business fare passengers receive complimentary
standard browsing on all WiFi-enabled flights.

### What entertainment is available?
All long-haul AeroNova aircraft are equipped with seatback screens offering a selection
of movies, TV shows, music, and games. The entertainment library is updated monthly.
On short-haul flights without seatback screens passengers may stream content to their
own device using the AeroNova Entertainment app, which must be downloaded before
boarding as in-flight connectivity may not support app downloads.

## Check-In and Boarding

### What time should I arrive at the airport?
For domestic flights AeroNova recommends arriving at least 90 minutes before departure.
For international flights AeroNova recommends arriving at least 3 hours before departure.
During peak travel periods such as public holidays and school breaks arriving an additional
30 to 60 minutes earlier is advisable.

### Can I check in at the airport if I already checked in online?
Yes. If you have checked in online and need to drop off checked baggage you should
proceed to the bag drop counters. If you have no checked baggage and have your boarding
pass you may proceed directly to security. Visiting the check-in desk after completing
online check-in is not necessary unless you have a special request or need to add baggage.

### What happens if I miss my flight?
If you miss your flight the outcome depends on your fare class. Basic fare passengers
forfeit their ticket with no refund or rebooking rights. Standard fare passengers lose
their ticket but may apply the value as a travel credit minus the $40 cancellation fee
if they contact AeroNova within 24 hours of the missed departure. Flex fare passengers
may rebook to the next available flight on the same route at no charge if they contact
AeroNova within 2 hours of the missed departure. Business fare passengers may rebook
to any available flight on the same route within 24 hours at no charge.

## Loyalty Program

### How do I join AeroRewards?
Membership is free and can be created on the AeroNova website or app. You will need
to provide your name, date of birth, email address, and a password. Once registered
your membership number can be added to existing bookings through Manage My Booking
to retroactively earn points on recent flights. Points can be claimed for flights
taken up to 30 days before the membership was created.

### My points did not appear after my flight. What should I do?
Points from flights are credited within 72 hours of the flight completing. If your
points have not appeared after 72 hours first check that your AeroRewards membership
number was included in the booking. If it was not you can add it retroactively for
flights taken within the last 30 days. If the number was included and points are still
missing contact AeroRewards support at rewards@aeronova.com with your booking reference
and flight details.

### Can I use points and cash together for a booking?
Yes. AeroNova allows combined points and cash payments. The minimum points redemption
is 5,000 points. Any remaining balance after points are applied must be paid in cash
using an accepted payment method. Taxes and fees cannot be covered by points and must
always be paid in cash.

## Disruptions and Complaints

### What are my rights if my flight is delayed?
For delays of 2 hours or more on flights under 1,500 km AeroNova will provide meal
vouchers to the value of $10. For delays of 3 hours or more on flights between 1,500
and 3,500 km meal vouchers to the value of $15 will be provided. For delays of 3 hours
or more on flights over 3,500 km meal vouchers to the value of $20 will be provided.
Hotel accommodation and transport will be provided if an overnight stay becomes necessary
due to the delay.

If the delay causes the passenger to arrive at their final destination 3 or more hours
later than originally scheduled and the delay was not caused by extraordinary circumstances,
compensation may be payable under applicable aviation regulations. Compensation amounts
mirror those applicable to flight cancellations as outlined in the rebooking and refunds
policy.

### How do I make a complaint?
Complaints can be submitted through the AeroNova website at www.aeronova.com/complaints,
by email to complaints@aeronova.com, or by post to AeroNova Customer Relations, PO Box
4471, New York, NY 10001. AeroNova aims to acknowledge all complaints within 48 hours
and provide a full response within 14 days. If you are not satisfied with AeroNova's
response you may escalate your complaint to the relevant aviation authority or an
approved alternative dispute resolution body in your country.

### What if AeroNova loses my important documents during travel?
If important documents such as a passport or visa are lost or damaged due to AeroNova's
handling, AeroNova will cover reasonable costs associated with obtaining emergency
replacements including emergency passport fees and rebooking costs if travel is delayed
as a result. Claims must be supported by documentation and submitted within 14 days
of the incident.
