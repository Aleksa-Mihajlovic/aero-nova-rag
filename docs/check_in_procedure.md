# AeroNova Airlines — Check-In Procedures

## Check-In Methods

AeroNova Airlines offers three ways to check in: online check-in, mobile app check-in,
and airport check-in. Passengers are strongly encouraged to use online or app check-in
to save time at the airport and avoid additional fees where applicable.

## Online and App Check-In

Online check-in opens 48 hours before the scheduled departure time and closes 2 hours
before departure for international flights and 1 hour before departure for domestic flights.
Passengers can check in via the AeroNova website at www.aeronova.com or through the
AeroNova mobile app available on iOS and Android.

During online check-in passengers can select or change their seat, add or pay for checked
baggage, upgrade their fare class subject to availability, and download or receive their
boarding pass by email or directly to their mobile wallet.

Passengers who have checked in online and have no checked baggage may proceed directly
to security without visiting the check-in desk. Passengers with checked baggage who have
completed online check-in may use the dedicated bag drop counters which typically have
shorter queues than standard check-in desks.

## Airport Check-In

Airport check-in desks open 3 hours before departure for international flights and
2 hours before departure for domestic flights. Check-in desks close 60 minutes before
departure for all flights without exception. Passengers who arrive at the check-in desk
after this deadline will not be accepted for travel and will forfeit their ticket unless
they hold a Flex or Business fare.

Standard and Basic fare passengers who miss the check-in deadline are not entitled to
a refund or rebooking. Flex fare passengers who miss the check-in deadline may rebook
to the next available flight on the same route at no charge, subject to seat availability.
Business fare passengers who miss the check-in deadline may rebook to any available
flight on the same route within 24 hours at no charge.

Self-service kiosks are available at all major AeroNova airports. Kiosks allow passengers
to check in, select seats, print bag tags, and print boarding passes. Kiosk check-in is
available from 3 hours before departure until 75 minutes before departure.

## Boarding Pass

Boarding passes are accepted in digital format on a mobile device or as a printed document.
AeroNova does not charge for printing boarding passes at the airport, however passengers
using self-service kiosks for printing at certain partner airports may be subject to a
local facility fee of up to $3 which is set by the airport operator and not by AeroNova.

Boarding passes contain the passenger name, flight number, departure and arrival airports,
departure date and time, seat number, boarding group, and a barcode or QR code for scanning
at security and the gate.

## Document Requirements

All passengers must present a valid government-issued photo ID at check-in. For domestic
flights a national ID card or passport is accepted. For international flights a valid
passport is required. Passengers traveling to destinations that require a visa must
present the relevant documentation at check-in. AeroNova staff will verify visa
requirements but it is the passenger's sole responsibility to ensure they hold the
correct documents for their destination.

Passengers who fail to present adequate documentation at check-in will be denied boarding
and will not be entitled to a refund unless the denial was caused by an error on the part
of AeroNova.

## Check-In for Special Passengers

Passengers requiring special assistance, including wheelchair assistance, traveling with
infants, or traveling with medical equipment, should notify AeroNova at least 48 hours
before departure by contacting customer support or updating their booking through Manage
My Booking. Special assistance passengers are advised to arrive at the airport at least
30 minutes earlier than the standard recommended arrival time.

Unaccompanied minors between the ages of 5 and 14 must be checked in at the airport
check-in desk regardless of whether online check-in has been completed. A parent or
guardian must accompany the child to the check-in desk and complete the required
unaccompanied minor form. A fee of $60 per flight applies for the unaccompanied minor
service.

## Boarding Procedure

Boarding begins 45 minutes before departure for international flights and 30 minutes
before departure for domestic flights. The gate closes 15 minutes before departure.
Passengers who have not boarded by gate closure will be offloaded and their checked
baggage will be removed from the aircraft.

Boarding groups are called in the following order: passengers requiring assistance and
families with children under 3, Business fare passengers, Flex fare passengers, AeroNova
loyalty program Gold and Silver members regardless of fare class, Standard fare passengers,
and finally Basic fare passengers.

Passengers must have their boarding pass and ID ready for scanning at the gate. Mobile
boarding passes must be fully loaded and visible before approaching the gate scanner.
Passengers are advised not to rely on loading their boarding pass while standing at the
gate as connectivity at gates may be limited.
