# AeroNova Airlines — Baggage Policy

## Carry-On Baggage

Every passenger is entitled to one carry-on bag and one personal item regardless of fare class.
The carry-on bag must not exceed 55 x 40 x 20 cm and must weigh no more than 10 kg.
The personal item, such as a laptop bag, handbag, or small backpack, must fit under the seat in front of you and must not exceed 30 x 20 x 15 cm.

Basic fare passengers may only store their personal item under the seat. Overhead bin access for carry-on bags is included from Standard fare and above. If a Basic fare passenger attempts to place a bag in the overhead bin at the gate, a fee of $30 will be charged before boarding.

## Checked Baggage Allowance by Fare Class

Basic fare includes no checked baggage. Passengers on Basic fare who wish to check a bag must purchase baggage separately during booking, during online check-in, or at the airport. Purchasing at the airport is significantly more expensive.

Standard fare includes one checked bag of up to 23 kg at no additional cost.

Flex fare includes two checked bags, each up to 23 kg, at no additional cost.

Business fare includes three checked bags, each up to 32 kg, at no additional cost. Business passengers also benefit from priority baggage handling, meaning their bags are among the first to appear on the carousel.

## Extra Baggage Purchase

Additional bags beyond the included allowance can be purchased at the following rates:
- Online during booking: $35 per bag per flight
- During online check-in (up to 4 hours before departure): $45 per bag per flight
- At the airport check-in desk: $65 per bag per flight
- At the gate: $85 per bag per flight

A maximum of 5 checked bags total per passenger per flight is allowed.

## Overweight and Oversized Baggage

Bags weighing between 23 kg and 32 kg are considered overweight and incur a fee of $50 per bag per flight, regardless of fare class. Bags weighing between 32 kg and 50 kg incur a fee of $90 per bag per flight. No single bag exceeding 50 kg is accepted for transport.

Bags exceeding 158 cm in total linear dimensions (length + width + height) are considered oversized and incur a fee of $75 per bag per flight. Items such as bicycles, surfboards, strollers, and golf equipment fall under the special items policy described below.

## Special Items

### Sports Equipment
Bicycles must be disassembled and packed in a hard-sided case. The fee is $60 per flight. Surfboards and similar items are charged at $55 per flight. Golf bags are charged at $45 per flight. Ski equipment including skis and boots packed together is charged at $50 per flight.

### Musical Instruments
Small instruments that fit within carry-on dimensions may be brought on board as a carry-on at no additional cost. Larger instruments such as cellos may be transported in the cabin if an additional seat is purchased. The cost of the additional seat equals the base fare for that flight. Instruments too large for the cabin must be checked and are subject to standard oversize fees.

### Strollers and Child Equipment
One stroller or pushchair per child may be checked free of charge regardless of fare class. Car seats and travel cribs are also accepted free of charge. These items should be tagged at the check-in desk and will be available for collection at the oversized baggage carousel upon arrival.

## Prohibited Items

The following items are strictly prohibited in both carry-on and checked baggage: explosives, flammable liquids exceeding 100 ml, lithium batteries above 160 Wh, weapons of any kind without prior written authorization, and live animals outside of the approved pet travel program.

Lithium batteries between 100 Wh and 160 Wh require prior approval and must be carried in the cabin only. Spare lithium batteries must always be in carry-on baggage and never in checked bags.

Liquids in carry-on baggage must follow the standard 100 ml rule. Each container must not exceed 100 ml and all containers must fit in a single transparent resealable bag of no more than one liter capacity.

## Liability and Claims

AeroNova Airlines accepts liability for checked baggage up to a value of $1,500 per passenger under standard conditions. Passengers wishing to declare a higher value must do so at check-in and pay an excess valuation fee of $0.50 per $100 of declared value above $1,500.

Claims for damaged baggage must be submitted before leaving the airport arrivals area. Claims for delayed baggage must be submitted within 21 days of the scheduled arrival date. Claims for lost baggage must be submitted within 21 days of the scheduled arrival date. AeroNova will consider baggage officially lost if it has not been located within 21 days of the report.
